✿ About Lost & Found Portal
Our values & how the platform works
01
Excellence ✦
We design a user-friendly platform for reporting and finding lost items. We believe in smart, secure, and intuitive experiences.
02
Collaboration ✧
The portal connects students, staff, and admins. Everyone contributes to reporting, verifying, and recovering lost items.
03
Transparency ✣
All reports and actions are tracked and visible to the relevant users. You’ll know the status at each step.
04
AI Assistance ✪
Admins can upload CCTV videos/images. Our AI model (YOLO) detects objects and helps match with reports quickly.
05
Smart Notifications ✺
Get instant dashboard alerts when items matching your report are detected or posted. No more missing updates.
06
Efficient Search ✤
Filter and search by keywords, date, location, and category. Finding or posting a lost item is just a few clicks away.
